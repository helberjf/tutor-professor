# The Blue Bird

Once upon a time, there was a little **bird**.
His name was Bluey.
Bluey was **blue**. He loved the **sky**.
One day, Bluey saw a **red** apple on a **green** tree.
"Hello!" said Bluey.
The apple was sweet.
Bluey was a happy bird!

---

**Vocabulary:**
- Bird: Pássaro
- Blue: Azul
- Sky: Céu
- Red: Vermelho
- Green: Verde
- Happy: Feliz
